EMOTIONAL INTELLIGENCE

What is emotional intelligence?

EQ and IQ are both measures of certain abilities. However, while the abilities they focus on work together, they are very different.

Main focusses of EQ:

  • identifying emotions
  • evaluating how others feel
  • controlling one's own emotions
  • perceiving how others feel
  • using emotions to facilitate social communication
  • relating to others

Main focusses of IQ:

  • visual and spatial processing
  • kKnowledge of the world
  • fluid reasoning
  • working memory and short-term memory
  • quantitative reasoning

Why is EQ important?

IQ and EQ are both elements in a complex array of influences that play roles in overall success, as well as health, wellness, and happiness. Emotional abilities (e.g. empathy, initiative and self-confidence) will be the main factor in determine whether someone is able to work well in a team. EQ will also influence the choices that consumers make when confronted with buying decisions i.e. sales success.
