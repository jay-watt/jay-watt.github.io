FOUNDATIONS REFLECTION

What are three new things you have learnt about yourself and your ego due to the core learning?

  1. My barrier to connecting and collaborating with others is more in my hands and less in the hands of the specific learning environment
  2. Listening is a skill I have difficulties with but one I can improve with practice
  3. It is very useful to explicitly write down goals and plans to achieve them in order to keep myself accountable, on-track and grounded

What are the role of values, empathy, and self-awareness in learning and programming?

Values, empathy and self-awareness are integral to interpersonal relationships whether professional or personal. If there is a shared willingness to understand each other, communication can be tailored to fit the individuals' needs. Only with effective communicating can individuals skills and knowledge be fully utilised to produce a superior end result. Ones values and levels of empathy and self-awareness contribute to the likelihood of understanding between two people with differing experience and backgrounds. In a pair programming, common ground may describe the ways collaborating that are comfortable for each person, i.e. regularity of breaks, in-person vs remote, regularity of switching roles, whether to have regular check-ins regarding the project's big picture, etc.

What has surprised you the most about the core learning?

It surprised me how much of the core content lined up with knowledge and practises that were on my mind prior to EDA. The neuroplasticity and growth mindset section, in particular, pointed me in the direction of resources that I feel I have been unconsciously looking for for some time. Also the focus on reflection and journaling has been a learning style that I had been making efforts to incorporate into my personal routine before starting study at EDA.

What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

Sharing blog posts with the cohort was very intimidating to me. I have felt quite anxious sharing my writing (tech and core) with such a large amount of people. I also found the listening and looping challenges easy for me to procrastinate through their perceived difficulty. However, after both listening and looping challenges, I was confident that the experiences had been positive and I had gained confidence from them.

Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development Bootcamp course?

I imagine that EDA focuses on core learning in order to give their students an edge and point of difference in the job market. Because of the benefits of working on code in pairs or groups, individuals with human skills are a valuable asset to any team that relies on collaborating. Part of the core content also works on making coding sustainable and enjoyable for the individual. EDA promotes this through putting an emphasis on growth and progress, rather than end products, as well as increasing our long-term learning potential through practicing reflection.

Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practise programming instead? Justify your answer.

The time spent on core throughout Foundations has not felt like a waste of time. While it took me a week or two to manage my stress enough to fully engage with the core content, the skills I have learnt will increase my longevity and effectiveness in programming long-term. Some of the practices I have started through the EDA core curriculum has also given me more tools to cope and grow in my personal life, the grounding from which also contributes to the likelihood of excelling in a professional programming environment. While it was easy to question the importance of the core content when directly comparing to the tech content, the indirect results of the core has given me access to code related knowledge I would not have had access to otherwise. Reaching out to peers and learning from them was made more accessible to me through completing core content.
